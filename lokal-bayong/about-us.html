Our Story

Luxe with a purpose.

Lokal Bayong reimagines the humble Filipino bayong as modern luxury — handwoven by local artisans, carried with pride.

The Beginning

A bag for a cause.

What began as a simple idea — to turn an everyday Filipino staple into something extraordinary — grew into a brand carried on runways, in boutiques, and across the country.

Each bag is woven entirely by hand from durable, upcycled materials. The result is a piece that is elegant, hard-wearing, and unmistakably ours: proudly Filipino, proudly handmade.

"Do what makes you happy. Every day is a struggle, but you have to be strong enough to face it."
— LJ Gaffud, Founder of Lokal Bayong
How it's made

Woven by hand, in Ifugao.

Every Lokal Bayong bag is woven entirely by hand by Indigenous Ifugao artisans. Watch the craft — and meet the people behind every weave.

What we stand for

Crafted with intention.

01

Handwoven

Every bag is woven by hand — never machine-made. Small imperfections are the signature of a human touch.

02

A livelihood

Each purchase provides fair, dependable income to our community of Filipino weavers and their families.

03

Made to last

Durable, upcycled materials mean a bag that is kind to the planet and built for everyday life.

The Cause

More than a bag.

When you carry Lokal Bayong, you carry the work of Filipino hands and the promise of a more sustainable, more equitable craft economy. That is luxe with a purpose — and a bag for a cause.
