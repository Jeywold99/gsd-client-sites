Our Story

A bag for a cause.

Lokal Bayong reimagines the humble Filipino bayong as a piece of modern luxury — handwoven by local artisans from durable, upcycled materials. Every order sustains a craft and a community.

What began as a small idea is now carried on runways, in boutiques, and across the country. Proudly Filipino, proudly handmade.

"Luxe with a purpose. Every bag we weave carries a story worth telling."
— LJ Gaffud, Founder
