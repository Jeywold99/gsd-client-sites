About Kissie Glaze

Where passion meets the perfect slice

The story behind the glaze

Kissie Glaze is a baker and cake artist with a deep passion for cake decorating and event curation. As an artist at heart, she fell in love with baking and curating unique cake designs — turning a creative calling into a thriving family business.

Beyond beautiful cakes, Kissie is driven to empower fellow artists to grow more reliable and confident in their craft. And with every order, one promise stays the same: your complete satisfaction.

Based in Bacoor, Cavite, Kissie Glaze brings handcrafted cakes, French pastries, and fresh florals to celebrations across the area.
